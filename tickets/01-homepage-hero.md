# Ticket 01: Homepage Hero

## Status
Completed

## Goal
Refine the homepage hero so it communicates identity, affiliation, and research direction with a calm editorial tone.

## Scope
- Hero only.
- No redesign of lower homepage sections unless required for spacing consistency.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Two-column desktop layout with text left and portrait right.
- Mobile stacks with portrait below text.
- Hero includes:
  - name
  - title and affiliation
  - research identity copy
  - actions for CV, Research, and Email
- No explicit job-market framing in the hero.
- Portrait is black and white, rectangular, and well cropped.
- Typography follows the agreed system.

## Constraints
- Keep the hero lifecycle-safe for pre-job-market and post-job-market use.
- Avoid startup-landing-page styling.
- Keep motion subtle and CSS-only where possible.
