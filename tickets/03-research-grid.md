# Ticket 03: Research Grid

## Goal
Turn the research page into a clean, modular papers/projects page with clear hierarchy between featured work, active work, and pipeline.

## Scope
- Research page layout and supporting styles.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Featured project appears first with strongest visual treatment.
- Additional projects appear in a clean grid.
- Each project card supports:
  - title
  - short summary
  - status/meta
  - optional coauthors or links
  - optional thumbnail
- Pipeline items are visually quieter than featured work.
- Card system feels precise and modular rather than blog-like.

## Constraints
- Maintain a skim-first academic tone.
- Avoid turning the page into a news feed or blog.
- Keep imagery consistent if placeholders are used.
