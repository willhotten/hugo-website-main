# Ticket 04: CV Page

## Goal
Refine the CV page into a skim-first academic profile page with the PDF as the dominant action.

## Scope
- CV page content structure and styling.

## Acceptance Criteria
- PDF download is clearly primary.
- Page supports a 15-second skim of:
  - current role
  - education
  - fields
  - contact
  - appointments
  - teaching
- Layout is typographic and calm.
- Remote logo dependencies are avoided.

## Constraints
- Do not make the page feel like an app dashboard.
- Avoid unnecessary tabs or heavy interaction unless clearly justified.
- Preserve low-maintenance editing in Markdown.
