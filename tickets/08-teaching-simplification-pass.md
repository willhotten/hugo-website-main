# Ticket 08: Teaching Simplification Pass

## Status
Completed

## Goal
Bring the teaching page into line with the rest of the site while removing the sense that it is a placeholder library awaiting content.

## Scope
- `content/teaching.md`
- supporting styles in `assets/css/custom.css`

## Acceptance Criteria
- Teaching page has a clear, credible academic purpose.
- Repeated placeholder signals such as `Slides forthcoming` are reduced or consolidated.
- Content and layout match the actual amount of teaching material currently available.
- Page remains easy to update as real slides are uploaded.

## Constraints
- Avoid overbuilding the page around materials that do not yet exist.
- Preserve a low-maintenance content structure.
- Prefer academic framing such as teaching experience, selected materials, or teaching topics over generic resource-library styling.
