# Ticket 05: Nav Microinteractions

## Goal
Polish the shared header and navigation so the chrome feels precise, quiet, and consistent with the site’s editorial/research-lab tone.

## Scope
- Header
- nav hover states
- footer polish if needed for consistency

## Acceptance Criteria
- Fixed header remains quiet and minimal.
- Active and hover states are clear and subtle.
- Underline or microinteraction feels engineered, not flashy.
- Footer is minimal and uncluttered.

## Constraints
- No heavy JS.
- No exaggerated motion.
- Preserve accessibility and strong contrast.
