# Ticket 07: Research Credibility Pass

## Status
Partial

## Goal
Make the homepage featured research and the research page read like credible academic outputs rather than polished placeholders.

## Scope
- `content/research.md`
- `content/_index.md`
- supporting styles in `assets/css/custom.css`

## Acceptance Criteria
- No dead or placeholder buttons remain on homepage featured research or the research page.
- Research items expose academically legible metadata where appropriate:
  - status
  - year if relevant
  - coauthors when confirmed
- Action language is standardised across homepage and research page.
- Research summaries use academically credible phrasing and avoid overclaiming.
- Thumbnails remain present but are visually secondary to titles and metadata.

## Constraints
- Keep the designed-card direction unless it clearly harms skim-readability.
- Do not add fake coauthors, dates, or outputs.
- Prefer `Draft available on request` or similar academic language over `coming soon`.
