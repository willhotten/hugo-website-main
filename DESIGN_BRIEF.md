# Design Brief

## Core Direction
This site should feel like:
- credible academic
- London design studio
- tech research lab

The working design ratio is:
- 70% rational grid, research-lab precision
- 30% editorial asymmetry and London design-studio tension

The site should not read like:
- a startup landing page
- an academic template
- a sales page for the job market

## Homepage Structure

### Section 1: Hero
- Identity-led, not paper-led.
- Two-column grid on desktop.
- Left column: name, title, affiliation, research identity sentence, actions.
- Right column: black-and-white portrait in square or 4:5 ratio.
- Mobile: stacked with portrait below text.

Content order:
1. Name
2. Title and affiliation
3. Research identity sentence
4. Actions:
   - CV (primary)
   - Research (secondary)
   - Email (text link)

Tone:
- calm
- confident
- not promotional

### Section 2: Research Agenda
- Three concise programme bullets.
- Structured and intentional.
- Not paragraph-heavy.

Agenda themes:
- empirical macro and firm heterogeneity
- innovation and the climate transition
- financial shocks, venture capital, and long-run outcomes

### Section 3: Featured Research
- Label: `Featured Research`
- Must be structurally renameable to `Job Market Paper` later without redesign.
- Single prominent card.

Include:
- title
- 2 to 3 line contribution summary
- status tag
- actions such as PDF, slides, code where available

### Section 4: Selected Projects Grid
- Two-column grid of additional papers or projects.
- Modular, clean, consistent card structure.

Each card should support:
- title
- one-line summary
- small meta line
- optional thumbnail image

## Typography
- Headings: `Fraunces`
- Body: `Inter`
- Meta and UI accents: `IBM Plex Mono`

Rules:
- serif only for headings and major titles
- body text should stay calm and legible
- avoid decorative type styling
- comfortable reading width around 65 to 75 characters

## Colour
Base:
- off-white background, approximately `#FAFAF8`
- near-black text, approximately `#111111` to `#1A1A1A`

Accent:
- restrained acid or VC green
- use only for links, tags, subtle underline accents, and hover states

Avoid:
- full green sections
- high-saturation blocks
- multiple accent colours

## Motion
- subtle underline animation on nav hover
- slight card elevation on hover
- no dramatic animation
- no heavy JS libraries

Motion should feel engineered, not flashy.

## Portrait Treatment
- black and white
- rectangular, not circular
- editorial crop
- no heavy drop shadow
- generous breathing room

## Footer
- minimal
- contact
- social icons
- copyright
- no theme credit clutter

## Lifecycle Rule
The homepage must function:
- pre-job-market
- during the job market
- post-job-market

Do not over-brand the current stage of the academic cycle.
